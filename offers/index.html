🌉Bridging Loans Made Easy

Want to buy before you sell?

A bridging loan can help you secure your next home before your current one is sold.

What is a Bridging Loan?

It's a short-term loan that "bridges the gap" between buying your new home and selling your current one. Your lender will take security over both properties until the process is complete.

  • ✅ Interest-only repayments during the bridging period
  • ✅ Access up to 100% of your new property's value (plus fees)
  • ✅ Capitalise the interest so you don't make repayments until your current home sells
Why Consider It?
  • 🏡 You can move quickly on a new home
  • 🛠️ Build your dream home while living in your current one
  • ⏳ Up to 6 months to sell (or 12 months if you're building)
  • 💡 Loan automatically switches to a standard mortgage after sale
Key Things to Think About
  • Do you have an offer on your current home?
  • Are you buying or building?
  • Will you live in the new property or rent it out?
  • Can you afford to service both loans during the transition?

Let's work out the best fit for you and minimise risk.

Capitalised Interest Bridging Loans
  • ✔ No repayments on the new loan during the transition
  • ✔ Interest builds into your loan instead of being paid monthly
  • ✔ Gives you breathing room to sell at the right time

✂️Split loans

Split Loans for Separate Buyers

Own a property together—keep your finances separate.

A split loan (not to be confused with fixed/variable) lets two or more people buy a home together while keeping their loan portions, repayments, and responsibilities separate. Perfect for people in non-traditional arrangements like friends, siblings, or separated couples who want to co-own a property but maintain financial independence.

How It Works

Each borrower takes out their own individual loan for their share of the property.

You're both on the property title, but each of you has:

  • ✔️ Your own loan
  • ✔️ Your own repayment schedule
  • ✔️ Your own interest rate (can be fixed or variable)
  • ✔️ Your own responsibility—no joint liability
Why People Choose Split Loans
  • ✅ Keep your finances independent
  • ✅ Protect your credit—you're not responsible if the other person misses repayments
  • ✅ Flexible loan terms—each person can choose what suits them
  • ✅ Perfect for co-buying with friends, family, or after separation
Is This Right for You?
  • Buying a home with someone you're not financially linked to
  • Want full control over your own portion of the loan
  • Want peace of mind that your credit and repayments are protected
